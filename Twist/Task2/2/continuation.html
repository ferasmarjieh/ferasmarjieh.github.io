Little Red Riding Hood

As she walked through the forest, Little Red Riding Hood met a wolf. The wolf asked her where she was going.

She hesitated, but then she said, "I’m going to visit my grandmother, who lives at the edge of the forest."

"Oh, what a lovely little girl you are," said the wolf. "Why don’t you pick some flowers for your grandmother?" Little Red Riding Hood thought it was a good idea and started to pick flowers.
